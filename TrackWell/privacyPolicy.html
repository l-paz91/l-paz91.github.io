TrackWell

Macro & lifestyle tracking, your way.

Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 2026

The short version: TrackWell is completely offline. Your data stays on your device. I can't see it, access it, or collect it.

What TrackWell Does

TrackWell helps you track macros (calories, protein, carbs, fat), weight, water intake, and supplements. It provides insights into your calorie balance and trends over time. All of this data is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All data you enter into TrackWell is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. As the developer, I have no way to access your information.

Data Export

You can export your data as a zip file containing JSON files. This export uses your phone's built-in sharing functionality – TrackWell itself doesn't access your file system or know where you save the file. You can import this zip file to restore your data.

Data Deletion

If you uninstall TrackWell, all app data is deleted from your device. There are no backups unless you've manually exported your data.

Permissions

TrackWell doesn't request access to your camera, files, contacts, location, or any other device features.

Third Parties

There are no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services in this app.
